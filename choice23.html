KEEP ENCOURAGING THE VILLAGERS TO FIGHT

"We have nothing left but to fight," you cry with conviction."

The villagers are hesitant.

Some of them decide to stay behind, but the majority agree to fight.

You arm them with swords and pickaxes and begin the long march back.

Suddenly, you hear the synchronized hoofbeats of the bandits, sounds that strike fear into everyone's hearts.

"How did they know we were coming?" you shout in terror.

Your friend next to you draws his sword. "I'm sorry, !, but I had to do this for all our sakes."

You cannot believe what you are hearing. Your friend has betrayed you. "Why?"

Suddenly, the hoofbeats stop. The bandits are here.
