TAKE A BREAK.

“I agree,” you say. “We should take a break now.”

Relieved, your friend immediately begins setting up his sleeping bag.

Before long, you are both asleep.

Fully rested, you and your friend continue your journey anew towards the sea.

Before long, you can see the shifting waves of the sea.

“Look!” you point. “I can see all the villagers!”

You and your friend run towards them in glee. However, you see their disheveled, bloody faces.

“Townspeople,” you step forward. “The bandits destroyed our homes and plundered our money. We must fight back for our rights and our lands.”

One villager shouts angrily. “No! We don’t want any more trouble, okay? Our wounded are still hurt.”

Several villagers nod seriously.

(1) KEEP ENCOURAGING THE VILLAGERS TO FIGHT.
(2) AGREE AND STOP ENCOURAGING THE VILLAGERS.
