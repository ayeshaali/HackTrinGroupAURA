THE VILLAGERS WAKE YOU UP.

You wake up in a soft bed, with the smell of the sea permeating through the air.

You rise, and after getting ready, you open the door and leave the hut you woke up in.

A young squire is waiting for you.

"There's a town meeting at the shore, do you want me to take you there?"
