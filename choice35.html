KILL YOUR FRIEND.

You stab Riffers through the heart.

Your friend looks on, horrified.

“Why did you do that? We defeated him!”

“He needed to die. Now we can be the leaders of these bandits”

“But…” You turn away from your friend before he finished.

“You bandits, I defeated your leader. Either challenge me or submit.”

The bandits look each other meekly and kneel before you.

“Now, to kill the remaining villagers is our goal.”

“Why would kill our friends?”

“We left behind anyway. Now we just need to finish the job, so that can’t go against us!”
