MAKE YOUR WAY TO THE HIDING BANDITS

As you walk to towards the bandits, you notice their regal clothes and the arrogant air about them.

You feel a whisper near your cheek and a cold blade against your neck

The man in the front of the group calls out “Who are you? What are you doing here?”

“Please… please…. I’ve dec… ided to…. join you!” you say in short gasps as the knife pressed harder against your neck.

Your friend appears behind you. “Please, let my friend go! We only wanted to join you”.

“Release him,” the man says. “I am Dread Pirate Riffers and the leader of these men. I am feared throughout the land for my ruthlessness and cunning.”

Your friend kneels before Riffers. You look down on your friend, angry at his submissiveness.

Riffers looks at you suspiciously. “Submit to me or die.”
