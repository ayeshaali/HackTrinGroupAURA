DO NOT SUBMIT.

You charge at him, Dread Pirate Riffers moves swiftly out of the way.

“You aren’t going to surrender?” he says as he pulls out a sword.

“Never!” You charge again, but you trip as he puts his foot out.

“Are you alright down there?” Riffers asks.

“Why would you care? I’m trying to kill you!!”

“I just want you to feel you’re doing well. I hate for people to die embarrassed.”

You get up and take a stab at him. You miss. You realize you are no match for him.

You see your friend out of the corner of your eye. He has a rock in his hand and slowly made his way around Riffers. The other bandits are too focused on the fight to see.

You charge again, but before you take a shot at him, your friend strikes his head with a rock. Riffers crumples to the ground.
