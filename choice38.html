TAKE THE BANDITS AND GO TOWARDS THE SEA.

“We will ride towards the sea now. Take your horses and prepare for battle.”

The bandits, with you in front, ride full speed, carrying spears and javelins.

You see the villagers huddled in the distance, holding their wounded. Easy targets.

You charge into battle, shouting at the top of your lungs, when you feel a spear at your back.

You turn around to meet the face of your friend.

“You...are betraying me?” you ask in shock.

Your friend replies, tears streaming down his face, “I have to. I have to save the people in my village.”

This betrayal is not worth your time. “Kill him,” you order the other bandits as you turn to the villagers.

The massacre is over as quickly as it begins. Wiping the blood of your sword, you smile.

THE END.
