KILL YOUR FRIEND.

You pull your sword out of your friend’s dead body.

Tears glisten in your eyes as you shout a rallying cry to the villagers.

The large number of villagers is no match for the overwhelmed bandits.

Finally, the Dread Pirate Riffers is on the floor, at your mercy. He growls in anger.

“Give up, bandit,” you say.

The pirate does nothing but spit on your face.

After the deed is done, you fall to the ground, suddenly weary. It was over.

The villagers huddled together. You stand up, planting your sword on the floor.

“Let’s go. It’s time to rebuild your village.”

THE END.
